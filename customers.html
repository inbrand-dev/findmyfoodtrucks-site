Find the truck before you drive

Find My Food Trucks will help customers discover active food trucks nearby, check current status, view recent updates, and search by city, cuisine, or truck name.

View demo truck Back to home

Live truck status

See whether a truck is open now, opening soon, offline, or recently active. The goal is to reduce guessing and wasted trips.

Search by what matters

Search by truck name, food type, city, or local area. Customers should be able to find BBQ, tacos, coffee, desserts, or whatever is nearby.

Recent activity

A simple feed will show location updates, menu notes, sold-out notices, upcoming stops, and other useful truck posts.

Favorites later

Future versions may let customers follow favorite trucks and quickly check where they are without hunting through social media.

MVP direction

Static shell now, live discovery later

This customer page is only a preview. The current MVP focus is the operator-side app. The website will become the discovery layer after backend sync is added.
